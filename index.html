EGUSI SOUP



Recipe details: Prep time: 15 min, Cook time: 45 min, Servings: 4, Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients list

  • 2 cups ground egusi (melon seeds)
  • 500g assorted meat & stockfish
  • 2 cups washed spinach or ugwu
  • 3 tbsp ground crayfish
  • 3 scotch bonnet peppers
  • 2 medium onions
  • ½ cup palm oil
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 cup meat stock
  • 1 tsp ground uziza seeds (optional)
  • Locust beans / iru (optional)

Instructions

  1. Season and boil the assorted meat and stockfish until tender. Reserve the stock.
  2. Blend the scotch bonnet peppers and one onion. Mix the ground egusi with a little water to form a thick paste.
  3. Heat palm oil in a pot over medium heat. Fry the sliced onion briefly, then add the pepper blend and fry for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the egusi paste in spoonfuls and fry, stirring frequently, until golden and fragrant about 10 minutes.
  5. Pour in the meat stock, add crayfish, seasoning cubes, and salt. Stir in the cooked meat. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  6. Fold in the spinach or ugwu leaves and cook for a final 3 - 5 minutes. Serve with pounded yam or eba.
Notes
  1. Frying the egusi paste thoroughly before adding stock removes the raw taste and builds deep flavour.
